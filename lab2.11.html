2.11.1 Write a definition for continuity on the half-open interval `(a,b]`.

`f` is continuous over `(a,b] iff f` is continuous over `(a,b)`, and is continuous from the left at `b`.

2.11.2 True or False? `h` is continuous on `[-4,-1)`.

true
